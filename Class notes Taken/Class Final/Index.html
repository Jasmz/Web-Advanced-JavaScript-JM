Aphelion

A PC/Mac/Linux game made by the students of DT: Danny Dang, Keiji Kimura, and Jasmine Martinez. Softwares used to make the game were Unity, CSharp language for scripting, as well as 3D modeling program Maya. Here's a little piece of how the story starts...

The Start of the Journey

This is the story of a wizardly father and his curious daughter.

The Separation

As they were one day together the little girl decided to wander off and encountered a faced mandrake that teleported her to some other realm separating her from her father.

Forest Wonders

The forest stands tall before her, colorful, as the girl looks around to find an exit back to her father.

The Wise One

She finds a helpful owl that tells her tips on how to continue.

The recipe

As she finds the ingredients, she puts the in the caulduron to hopefully transfrom into a frog, and cross the forest safely.
